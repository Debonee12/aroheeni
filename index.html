About AROHEENI

Founded in 2010, AROHEENI is a non-profit organization committed to empowering rural women across India. We believe that when women are given the right tools and opportunities, they can transform not only their own lives but also their families and communities.

Our work focuses on four key areas: education, health, skill development, and self-help groups. Through these initiatives, we aim to break the cycle of poverty and create sustainable change in rural India.

With a team of dedicated professionals and volunteers, we've reached over 50 villages across 5 states, impacting the lives of thousands of women and their families.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a society where every rural woman has access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities, enabling her to live with dignity and self-reliance.

We envision a future where rural women are not just beneficiaries but leaders of change in their communities. Through our holistic approach, we address multiple dimensions of empowerment:

  • Education for girls and adult literacy programs
  • Healthcare access and awareness
  • Vocational training and skill development
  • Financial literacy and self-help groups
  • Leadership and entrepreneurship training
